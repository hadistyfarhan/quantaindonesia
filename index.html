About Us

Indonesia's Trusted
Maritime Partner

Est. 2000
Professional Tug & Barge Operations — Indonesian Waters

Professional Tug & Barge Services Across the Archipelago

PT Quanta Indonesia (QI) is a shipping company specializing in sea transportation using barges towed by tugboats. QI was established in 2000, and in June 2024 was taken over by new management and organizational structure.

Following the transition, QI began operations by chartering 2 (two) sets of Tug Boat and Barge (Time Charter). In January 2025, QI expanded its charter fleet to 3 (three) active sets — reflecting strong demand and growing operational capacity.

We operate under SOLAS international safety standards, ensuring every shipment upholds the highest benchmarks of safety for vessel, cargo, crew, and the environment.

Our Services

Integrated Maritime
Solutions

Comprehensive shipping services supporting Indonesia's energy, cement, mining, and industrial sectors.

01

Tug & Barge Charter

Time charter services for Tug Boat and Barge sets — our core business. Currently operating 3 active charter sets serving energy and industrial clients across Indonesian waters.

02

Domestic Sea Transport

Inter-island cargo transport using our owned and chartered fleet, fully operating under SOLAS safety standards across the Indonesian archipelago.

03

Mother Vessel — General Cargo

General cargo transport using Mother Vessel for larger volume shipments requiring higher capacity and longer sea routes between major Indonesian ports.

04

Stone Trading

Supply and trading of quality stone materials from trusted sources: Hamparan, Bosowa, and Watusampu — delivered reliably to your project site.

05

Cargo Handling

Professional loading and unloading operations with experienced stevedoring teams and standard equipment at major Indonesian ports.

06

Vessel Repair & Maintenance

Full maintenance and repair services for tugboats and barges, performed by certified marine technicians with comprehensive equipment support.
